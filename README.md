## Australian Bond Market Liquidity Impact of RBA Decisions

This project analyses the impact of Reserve Bank of Australia cash rate decisions on ASX 10-Year Bond Futures liquidity.

### Key Findings
- Average trading volume on RBA decision days is **1.41x higher** than normal trading days.
- Significant liquidity spikes are observed immediately following policy announcements.

### Tools Used
- Python, pandas, matplotlib
- RBA cash rate target data
- ASX 10Y bond futures historical volume data

### Methodology
1. Cleaned and standardized RBA policy rate time-series data.
2. Processed ASX bond futures volume data.
3. Identified RBA policy change dates.
4. Measured liquidity spikes by comparing trading volumes on RBA days vs normal days.
